How to Still Be Reminded of Your Insignificance Even When Light Pollution Makes It Impossible to See the Stars







*
Consider the vastness of the light-pollution problem.
If you need something big to make you feel small, think about the issue of light pollution itself.
It’s huge—almost as huge as the number of stars in the night sky. And you’re so insignificant that there’s basically nothing you can do to stop it.
*
*
You could keep all your lights off at night, blindly fumbling your way through your pitch-dark apartment, but the stars outside would remain just as invisible as they are now.
Take a deep breath.
You’re just one helpless person out of billions, and that’s a beautiful thing.
